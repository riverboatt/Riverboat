[Intro – spoken]

I came back with tomorrow folded in my coat.
Not to warn you. To recruit you.
History isn't fixed. It's under construction.

[Verse 1]

I have walked where your grandchildren sing
On a mountain built from everything
You were told was done, discarded, dead—
One hundred fifty million tons instead.
Freshkills wasn't failure. It was proof:
Every ruin is a waiting roof.
Civilizations hide their guilt below;
Engineers return to make it grow.

I have seen the landfill bloom with light,
Concerts underground through hurricane nights.
The Earth becomes cathedral, cool and bright.
Science is what hope looks like.

[Pre-Chorus]

Don't bury the blueprint.
Bury the excuse.
Matter never quits—
Only human use.

[Chorus]

Build it better somewhere else.
That's the future. That's the spell.
Open every drawing. Share the code.
Knowledge multiplies when freely told.
Waste becomes a garden. Steel becomes a song.
Wrong arrangement—that's what made it wrong.
Leave each place beyond the one you knew.
Tomorrow isn't found. It's built by you.

[Verse 2]

Slides descend instead of marble stairs;
Wonder is infrastructure wearing flair.
Solar roofs feed batteries below,
Water falls from towers as the trade winds blow.
Every pipe behind transparent glass,
Every watt accounted as you pass.
Museums made of systems still alive—
Nothing hidden just to look more wise.

Children grow tomorrow with their hands,
Printing tools instead of buying brands.
Every meal is measured, every seed has names,
Every failure archived without shame.

[Bridge – spoken / sung]

I have met the inventors history misplaced:
Tesla. Lamarr. Clark. A thousand nameless faces.
Their reward was not applause.
It was possibility.
Don't worship us. Continue us.

[Final Chorus]

Build it better somewhere else.
Copy every single thing that helps.
Open source the miracle. Improve the plan.
The future isn't owned by any man.
Ten thousand landfills waiting for a spark,
Ten thousand cities waiting in the dark.
Turn the scar into the city's heart;
That's where every revolution starts.

[Outro]

I'm not here to save your century.
I'm here because you may yet save mine.
Every future is a question,
Answered by each generation.

[End]
Write the answer.
